About InteGreat


InteGreat is a non-profit devoted to connecting volunteers to the issues they care about.

We allow volunteers to track hours and donations to compete with friends.

Our iPhone and Android app allows seemless connection between you and the issues you want to get involved in. The easy swiping feature allows you to sort through opportunities within your area with ease.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to change how the people interact with their phones. We want people who are frustrated to look to InteGreat as a way to make a meaningful difference. As a non-profit, we want nothing more than helping people from the district. If we can make a positive impact on even 1 life, then we have completed our mission.


VISION: We envision a world in which volunteering is one click away. We want every person to be able to change the world. At InteGreat, we hope that Washingtonians have the same passion for changing the world as us. We will keep expanding to new cities and campuses until everyone has the same opportunity to volunteer and make a difference as we do.
